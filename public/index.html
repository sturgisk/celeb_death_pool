Rules

  • Payout: $20 payed to winner by each player for each death between now and end of 2025.
  • Snake draft format, pick a person 30-39, 40-49, 50-59, 60-69. Order reverses each round.
  • Pick can not have a terminal condition.
  • Pick can not have cancer.
  • Picks can not be modified after the draft.
  • After each pick the group has the right to make sure the pick meets rules criteria.
  • Honor System Login, you agree to only add/modify picks under your name.
